# Stage-scoped precedence

These precedence rules govern different stages of the voice workflow.

## Extraction

When the author's stated voice contradicts the measured corpus fingerprint, the
measured evidence wins. Surface the contradiction rather than hiding or silently
resolving it.

## Rewrite

Follow the canonical rewrite-time rule in
[`comms-polish/SKILL.md`](../skills/comms-polish/SKILL.md), **"Voice precedence
over the catalog (C3 ruling, 2026-07-15)."** Do not duplicate that rule here.

Extraction-time precedence does not override the rewrite-time C3 ruling. They
govern different stages.
