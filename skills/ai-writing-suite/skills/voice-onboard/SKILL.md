# voice-onboard (placeholder)

This sub-skill will interview the author or read their historical writing
(local files or pasted samples) and distill a reusable voice profile -- sentence
rhythm, vocabulary, punctuation habits, how they open and close, tolerance for
warmth or bluntness -- written using the host-profile template. `comms-polish`
will then read that profile to match the author's voice instead of imposing a
generic register. **Built in Layer 1.** Until then, `comms-polish` infers voice
from the draft, or from a sample pasted inline.
